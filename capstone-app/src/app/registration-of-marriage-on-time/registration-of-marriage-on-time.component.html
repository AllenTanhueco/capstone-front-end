On-Time Registration of Marriage

DOCUMENT PREVIEW
Marriage Certificate (MF 102) Must be registered within 15 days after solemnization
LIST OF REQUIREMENTS
  • Mandatory Document
  • Duly accomplished and signed Marriage Certificate (MF 102)
  • Who May File
  • Solemnizing Officer
  • Married couple (husband or wife)
  • Parents
  • Process Highlights
  • 1. Submit document signed by Solemnizing Officer (5 mins)
  • 2. Verify entries and completeness (Included in Step 1)
  • 3. Review and sign by City Civil Registrar (2 mins)
  • 4. Assignment of Registry Number (2 mins)
  • 5. Release of registered document (1 min)
Application Fee: None
Processing Time: 10 Minutes
