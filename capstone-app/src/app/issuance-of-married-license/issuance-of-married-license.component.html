Issuance of Marriage License

DOCUMENT PREVIEW
Marriage License Application Valid for 120 days upon issuance
LIST OF REQUIREMENTS
  • Core Requirements
  • One party must be a resident of Angeles City
  • Certificate of Live Birth (both parties)
  • PSA CENOMAR (CRS Form 4)
  • Attendance: Responsible Parenthood Seminar
  • Age-Specific Requirements
  • 18-20 years old: Parental Consent & Pre-Marriage Counseling
  • 21-24 years old: Parental Advice & Pre-Marriage Counseling
  • Special Cases
  • Previously Married: Annulment Decree or Death Certificate
  • Foreign Applicant: Certificate of Legal Capacity to Marry
  • The Process
  • Step 1: Submission & Interview (20 mins)
  • Step 2: Payment at Windows 13-19 (5 mins)
  • Step 3: 10-Day Posting Period
  • Step 4: Present Seminar Certs & Claim License
Application Fee: ₱250.00
Processing Time: 10 Days Posting
