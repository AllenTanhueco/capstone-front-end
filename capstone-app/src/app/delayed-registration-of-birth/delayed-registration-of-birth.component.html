LIST OF REQUIREMENTS
  • Mandatory Documents
  • Duly accomplished and signed Certificate of Live Birth
  • PSA Certificate of No Registration
  • For Adults (18-59 years old)
  • Any two: Baptismal, Voter's Certification, Form 137, SSS E-1, or Passport
  • For Minors (under 18)
  • Baptismal Certificate and School Record (Form 137)
  • Medical Certificate or Immunization Card
  • If Parents are Unmarried
  • Affidavit of Admission of Paternity
  • Affidavit to Use the Surname of the Father (RA 9255)
