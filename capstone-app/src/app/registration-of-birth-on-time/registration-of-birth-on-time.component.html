On-Time Registration of Birth

DOCUMENT PREVIEW
Certificate of Live Birth Must be registered within 30 days of birth
LIST OF REQUIREMENTS
  • Mandatory Document
  • Duly accomplished and signed Certificate of Live Birth
  • For Unmarried Parents
  • Affidavit of Acknowledgment/Admission of Paternity
  • Affidavit to Use the Surname of the Father (RA 9255)
  • Both parents must be present during registration
  • Fee Breakdown (Unmarried)
  • Affidavit to Use Surname of Father: ₱300.00
  • 3 Certifications (₱50.00 each): ₱150.00
  • UP Law Center (UPLC): ₱10.00
  • Who May File
  • Hospital Representative, Attendant at Birth, or Parents
Application Fee: ₱0.00 - ₱460.00
Processing Time: 10 - 30 Minutes
